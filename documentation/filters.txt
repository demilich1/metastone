Filters

AndFilter
Combines multiple filters.

AttributeFilter
Filters based on the specific attribute given.

CardFilter
Filters based on a multitude of card attributes.

DamagedFilter
Filters entities if they are damaged or not.

HighestAttributeFilter
Filters entities based on which entity has the highest value of the attribute given.

InDeckFilter
Filters based on whether it belongs to the gamePlayer's deck.

RaceFilter
Filters based on the Race given.

SpecificCardFilter
Filters based on the specific card name provided.
